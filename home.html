🏺 Traditional Bilona Method 🥛

🌟 Our Sacred 6-Step Traditional Process 🌟

1 🐄 Fresh Cow Milk Collection

Premium milk from healthy, grass-fed indigenous cows, collected during optimal milking hours for maximum nutrition.

2 🥛 Natural Curd Formation

Overnight fermentation using traditional starter culture (jamun) for 8-12 hours at optimal temperature.

3 🏺 Sacred Bilona Churning

Hand-churning with wooden bilona (traditional churner) in clockwise direction for 45-60 minutes to separate pure butter.

4 🔥 Gentle Heating Process

Slow heating of butter on low flame using traditional copper/brass vessels, carefully monitoring temperature to preserve nutrients.

5 🧈 Pure Ghee Extraction

Careful separation of golden ghee from milk solids when aromatic fragrance peaks, ensuring maximum purity.

6 🏺 Traditional Cooling

Natural cooling and crystallization in clay pots, allowing proper texture development and flavor maturation.
